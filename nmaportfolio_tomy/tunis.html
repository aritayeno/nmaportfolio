TUNIS

Logo

The Goal

Design a logo for Tunis to reflect it's history and culture.

The Solution

Jasmine—the national flower of Tunisia, is the focal point of this logo. The lines surrounding the flower respresent the ripple-effect caused by the outcome of the Tunisian Revolution. The subtle colors mimic mosaic art—Tunisia's most well-known art form.
